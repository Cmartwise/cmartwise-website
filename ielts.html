The exam

What is IELTS Academic?

IELTS (International English Language Testing System) Academic is the standard English proficiency test for university admissions, skilled-worker visas, and professional registration worldwide — scored on a 9-band scale.

What it tests
4 sections
Listening · Reading · Writing · Speaking
Two academic reading passages under time pressure, a graph/data description plus an essay for Writing, a face-to-face interview for Speaking, and four recorded Listening sections of increasing difficulty. Timing is tight everywhere — pacing is half the battle.
Who needs it
University & visa applicants
Admissions · Skilled-worker visas · Professional registration
If a university, employer, or immigration authority asked for an IELTS Academic score, this is almost certainly the one. Most require a specific overall band plus minimums per section — I help you hit both.

Which exam do you need?

CIPLE and DIPLE — what's the difference

Both are official CAPLE certifications from the University of Lisbon. Which one you need depends on your situation.

Most common
CIPLE
A2 · Elementary Portuguese
The standard requirement for Portuguese citizenship through naturalization. Tests everyday situations — shops, appointments, basic conversations.

The 6-week programme (€600) takes you from zero to ready for this exam. No prior Portuguese needed.
Higher level
DIPLE
B1 · Intermediate Portuguese
Required for some work visas and professional certifications, or chosen by people who want a stronger proof of language ability.

The 8-week programme (€750) takes you from A2 level through to B1 and exam-ready. You need some basic Portuguese to start.

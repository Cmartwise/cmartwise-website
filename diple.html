The exam

What is the DIPLE?

DIPLE (Diploma Intermédio de Português Língua Estrangeira) is an official B1-level certification from the University of Lisbon — one level above CIPLE. It proves intermediate command of Portuguese in professional and everyday contexts.

What it tests
4 components
Reading · Listening · Writing · Speaking
At B1 level, the exam covers longer texts, more complex listening tasks, structured written responses, and a real dialogue with an examiner. The writing and oral sections carry significant weight — and that's where focused preparation pays off most.
Who needs it
Beyond citizenship
Work visas · Professional credentials · Higher certification
DIPLE is required for certain work authorizations and professional bodies, or chosen by expats who want a more meaningful proof of language ability than A2. You need some existing Portuguese (A2 level) to start this programme.
